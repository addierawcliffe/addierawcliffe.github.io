All About Gilmore Girls!

Gilmore Girls is a light-hearted show. It follows "The Gilmore Girls" known as Lorelai, Rory, and Emily Gilmore. Their lives are messy, complicated, and nothing short of entertaining. The show takes place in the town of Stars Hollow, where Lorelai 'escaped' to, to get away from the rich and 'proper' way of life her parents live in and tried to force her into too. When she gets pregnant at 16, she is expected to marry Rory's father, but Lorelai has other plans, she follows her dreams of running her own inn and when the intellegent Rory gets accepted to CHilton, a private shcool, Lorelai can't afford it. She must ask her parents, and they come up with a deal, Richard and Emily Gilmore willl pay for Chilton, but Lorelai and Rory must go to weekly Friday night dinners at thier house. And it all begins...
